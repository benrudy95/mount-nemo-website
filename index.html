Our Story

Fifty Years of
Championship Golf

Since 1973, Mount Nemo Golf Club has been one of Burlington's most beloved golfing destinations — offering a true championship experience at remarkable value.

Set against the breathtaking backdrop of the Niagara Escarpment, our Par 71 layout winds through rolling terrain and mature tree-lined fairways. Six water hazards challenge you on the front nine, while the back nine rewards accuracy through narrow corridors of deep forest. From the 11th tee, golfers are treated to a spectacular view of the Toronto skyline — one of the most iconic moments in Ontario golf.
